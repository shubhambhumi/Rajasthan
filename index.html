Our 5-Day Road Trip Itinerary

Day 1: Oct 22nd - Ahmedabad to Udaipur

Travel: Drive from Ahmedabad to Udaipur (approx. 260 km, 5-6 hours).
Itinerary: Arrive in Udaipur. Check in and relax for about an hour. In the evening (3-4 hours), explore the beautifully lit City Palace and enjoy a boat ride on Lake Pichola to view the Diwali lights.

Day 2: Oct 23rd - Exploring Udaipur

Itinerary: Spend a full day in Udaipur. Visit the majestic City Palace (approx. 2-3 hours), the serene Jagdish Temple (approx. 1 hour), and the charming Saheliyon Ki Bari (approx. 1-2 hours). The rest of the day is free for shopping or exploring.

Day 3: Oct 24th - Udaipur to Mount Abu

Travel: Drive from Udaipur to Mount Abu (approx. 180 km, 3-4 hours).
Itinerary: Check out after breakfast. Arrive in Mount Abu in the afternoon. Relax at the hotel for a bit (approx. 1 hour). In the evening (approx. 2-3 hours), head to Sunset Point for a breathtaking view and then enjoy a leisurely boat ride on Nakki Lake.

Day 4: Oct 25th - Mount Abu Exploration

Itinerary: Spend a full day exploring Mount Abu. Visit the stunning Dilwara Jain Temples (approx. 2-3 hours) in the morning. Then drive to Guru Shikhar (approx. 1 hour drive each way + 1-2 hours at the top) for panoramic views. Visit the Brahma Kumaris Peace Park (approx. 1 hour) for a peaceful break.

Day 5: Oct 26th - Mount Abu to Ahmedabad

Travel: Drive back from Mount Abu to Ahmedabad (approx. 230 km, 5-6 hours).
Itinerary: Check out of your hotel in the morning. Begin the journey back to Ahmedabad. You can stop at the Ambaji Temple on the way back for a final stop before reaching home.
