Our 5-Day Road Trip Itinerary

Day 1: Oct 22nd - Ahmedabad to Udaipur

Start the journey from Ahmedabad in the morning. Enjoy the scenic drive to Udaipur. Check into the hotel and get ready for a magical evening. Explore the illuminated City Palace and take a romantic boat ride on Lake Pichola to see the beautiful lights.

Day 2: Oct 23rd - Exploring Udaipur

Spend the day sightseeing in Udaipur. Visit the majestic City Palace, the peaceful Jagdish Temple, and the beautiful Saheliyon Ki Bari. The day is yours to explore the city's rich history and culture.

Day 3: Oct 24th - Udaipur to Mount Abu

After a relaxed morning in Udaipur, drive to Mount Abu, the only hill station in Rajasthan. Check into your hotel and then head out to watch a breathtaking sunset from Sunset Point.

Day 4: Oct 25th - Mount Abu Exploration

This day is all about Mount Abu's top sights. Visit the stunning Dilwara Jain Temples, known for their intricate carvings. Drive up to Guru Shikhar for panoramic views and find inner peace at the Brahma Kumaris Peace Park. In the evening, take a leisurely boat ride on Nakki Lake.

Day 5: Oct 26th - Mount Abu to Ahmedabad

Enjoy one last breakfast in Mount Abu. Begin the journey back to Ahmedabad. You can stop at the Ambaji Temple on the way back for a final stop before reaching home, filled with wonderful memories.
